## Tips

1. **Be Clear**: Use simple commands like "open chrome" or "chrome kholo"
2. **Use Keywords**: Include action words like "open", "close", "kholo", "band"
3. **App Names**: Use common names like "chrome", "notepad", "calculator"

## Troubleshooting

**App not opening?**
- Check if app is installed
- Try full name: "google chrome" instead of "chrome"

**Command not working?**
- Use clear patterns: "open [app]" or "[app] kholo"
- Avoid complex sentences

**Voice not working?**
- Check microphone permissions
- Click "Calibrate Voice" button
- Speak loudly and clearly
